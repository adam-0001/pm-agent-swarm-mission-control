FocusFlow is an AI-powered deep work assistant for remote knowledge workers. It integrates with calendar, Slack, and browser to automatically detect context switches, block distractions during focus sessions, and generate end-of-day productivity reports. Target market is remote software engineers, designers, and product managers at companies with 50-500 employees who struggle with meeting overload and constant Slack interruptions. The core problem: knowledge workers lose 2-3 hours per day to context switching and interruptions, but existing tools (Freedom, Cold Turkey, RescueTime) only block websites — they don't understand work context or integrate with team communication tools.
